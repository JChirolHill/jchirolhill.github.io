Why Choose the Eclipse Method

At some point our lives, most of us have wanted to learn a language but unfortunately, most of us fell victim to these myths:

  • Only kids can learn languages
  • You can't work full time and learn a language
  • I've already spent years in high school and in college trying to learn this language, but I'm still not fluent
  • I don't have the "language gene"

Fortunately, none of these myths are accurate. Learning a language is more a matter of better organizing one's time with the tips I give you on this site and finding the right resources to design a learning style unique to you.

This website will guide you to meet these goals and help you achieve your language learning dreams.
